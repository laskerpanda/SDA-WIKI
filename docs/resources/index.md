# Resource Bank

**SDA Commons Wiki · South High School**

The Resource Bank is the raw materials library for SDA. Everything here is a source document — PDFs, framers, unit plans, student samples, primary sources — organized by subject and ready to be processed into wiki entries, skinnies, or reading library pages.

---

## How This Works

The Resource Bank is modeled on the same pattern as the Continental Philosophy Wiki's raw library. Drop a file in the right folder, and Hermes can process it into a full wiki entry:

1. Place a PDF or document in the appropriate subfolder below
2. Tell Hermes: `"Process RAW_INTAKE/[folder]/[filename] into a reading library entry"`
3. Hermes reads the file, generates a structured wiki page, and commits it

## Folders

### Primary Sources
[Primary Sources](primary-sources/index.md) — Historical documents, speeches, legal texts, manifestos. The raw texts that become reading library entries.

### FPS Framers
[FPS Framers](fps-framers/index.md) — South High ELA and Social Studies unit framers. The official curriculum documents that define what students are expected to know.

### Student Samples
[Index](/resources/student-samples/index.md) — Anonymized student work at each proficiency level (1.0–4.0). Used to calibrate rubrics and train Hermes to score accurately.

### Studio Archives
[Index](/resources/studio-archives/index.md) — Completed studio documentation from previous years. Essential Questions, Demos, Deliverables, and outcomes.

### Unit Plans
[Index](/resources/unit-plans/index.md) — World History and US History unit plans, era guides, and pacing documents.

### Misc
[Index](/resources/misc/index.md) — Everything else that doesn't fit cleanly above.

## Processing Queue

Files dropped in `~/sda-wiki/RAW_INTAKE/` are the processing queue. Subfolders:

| Folder | What Goes Here |
|--------|---------------|
| `RAW_INTAKE/reading_library/` | PDFs to become reading library entries |
| `RAW_INTAKE/skinnies_raw/` | Raw standard descriptions to become skinnies |
| `RAW_INTAKE/fps_frames/` | FPS framer documents |
| `RAW_INTAKE/standards/` | Standards documents |
| `RAW_INTAKE/crashcourse/` | CrashCourse transcripts |
| `RAW_INTAKE/misc/` | Everything else |
