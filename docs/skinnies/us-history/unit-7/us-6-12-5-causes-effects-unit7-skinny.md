# Skinny — US.6_12.5 Causes & Effects · Unit 7 Vietnam Era

**Standard:** US.6_12.5 — Causes and Effects of Historical Events
**Unit:** 7 — Vietnam Era & Watergate (1964–1974)
**Grade:** 10 · US History

---

## What This Standard Asks

What caused US involvement in Vietnam? What caused Watergate? What did each produce?

---

## Causes of US Vietnam Involvement

| Cause | Explanation |
|-------|-------------|
| Containment doctrine | US committed to stopping communism globally since Truman |
| Domino theory | Fear Southeast Asia falls if Vietnam goes communist |
| French colonial war ends (1954) | US steps in after France leaves; supports South Vietnam's Diem government |
| Gulf of Tonkin (1964) | Reported attack on US ships (later found exaggerated); Congress authorizes war |
| Political cost of "losing" | No president wanted to be the one who "lost" Vietnam |
| Military credibility | US had to show it could win to deter Soviet aggression elsewhere |

## Effects of Vietnam

| Effect | Significance |
|--------|-------------|
| 58,000 Americans dead; 2+ million Vietnamese | Human cost |
| Public trust in government collapses | "Credibility gap" → permanent skepticism |
| War Powers Resolution (1973) | Congress tries to limit presidential war-making |
| Military volunteers only (draft ends 1973) | Draft causes too much domestic unrest; all-volunteer military |
| Vietnam syndrome | US reluctance to commit ground troops for decades |
| Pentagon Papers reveal systematic deception | Media-government relationship permanently changed |

## Causes and Effects of Watergate

| Stage | Event |
|-------|-------|
| Cause | Nixon's paranoia; "dirty tricks" campaign; plumbers unit |
| Event | June 1972 break-in at DNC headquarters |
| Escalation | Cover-up: hush money, FBI interference, obstruction of justice |
| Unraveling | Senate hearings; tapes; Saturday Night Massacre; *US v. Nixon* |
| Effect | Nixon resigns; 40 aides convicted; campaign finance reform; inspector general offices |

---

## The Argument Move

US.6_12.5 in Unit 7 is about compounding effects — one event making another possible.

**Example argument move:**
> "Watergate was caused by Vietnam. Nixon's 'plumbers unit' was created to stop leaks about the war — specifically the Pentagon Papers. The same people who broke into the DNC had been doing illegal intelligence work related to Vietnam. If there had been no Pentagon Papers, there might have been no plumbers. If there had been no plumbers, there might have been no Watergate. The two events aren't separate stories."
