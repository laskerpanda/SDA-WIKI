## What This Standard Asks

What from the Cold War is still present today? How does understanding this period explain current geopolitics and domestic politics?

## The Argument Move

US.6_12.6 in Unit 5 is about Cold War frameworks returning.

**Example argument move:**
> "Analysts increasingly describe US-China competition as a 'new Cold War.' The comparison is partly accurate and partly misleading. Accurate: two superpowers with incompatible systems competing for global influence. Misleading: the US and China are economically interdependent in ways the US and USSR never were — which makes the conflict more complicated and the stakes different. Understanding the original Cold War helps you see both why the comparison is tempting and where it breaks down."
