# Grade 10 — ELA & US History Crosswalk

**South High School · Self-Directed Academy · Grade 10**

This tab is the integration hub for Grade 10 SDA. Every Studio in Year 2 combines an English standard with a US History standard. This section organizes all wiki resources — reading library texts, CrashCourse episodes, skinnies, and studio packets — by unit group, so you can see everything relevant to one Studio in one place.

> **How to use this:** Find the unit your Studio is in. Everything on that page connects to each other.

---

## How Grade 10 Works

In Grade 10, you are simultaneously enrolled in **English II** and **US History**. The integration works the same way as Grade 9 — one Studio, two standards, one body of evidence. The content shifts to American history (Reconstruction to present) but the ELA skills you built in Grade 9 carry forward and deepen.

**The standard pairing pattern every Studio:**

| Slot | Standard | Notes |
|------|----------|-------|
| **US History** | One USH standard (US.6_12.1–6) + one Unit | The content anchor |
| **Reading** | R.2, R.4, R.7, R.8, or R.9 | Primary source + text analysis |
| **Writing** | W.3 (explanatory) or W.4 (argument) | Alternates each Studio |
| **Research** | IR.1–5 bundle | Active every Studio |
| **Communication** | C.1 and/or C.6 | Assessed at Week 6 exhibition |
